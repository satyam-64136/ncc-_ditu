About 29 UK Battalion

The NCC builds character, discipline and leadership in India's youth through rigorous military-style training.

🎯
Mission

To develop character, comradeship, discipline, leadership, secular outlook, the spirit of adventure, and ideals of selfless service among the youth of India.

⚔️
Training

Rigorous military drills, weapon training, map reading, first aid, adventure activities, and participation in national-level camps and Republic Day parades.

🏆
Excellence

Our cadets consistently earn A, B and C certificates and compete at state and national levels, with many going on to serve in the Indian Armed Forces.
