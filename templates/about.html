NATIONAL CADET CORPS
INDIA

Established in 1948, the NCC is the largest uniformed youth organisation in the world. The 29 UK Battalion at DIT University, Dehradun carries this proud legacy forward across Uttarakhand.

// 29 UK BN

THE BATTALION

The 29 Uttarakhand Battalion is stationed in Dehradun and encompasses multiple colleges in the Garhwal region. DIT University's NCC unit trains cadets in Army Wing activities including drill, weapon handling, map reading, and field craft.

Our cadets regularly participate in Republic Day parades, Annual Training Camps (ATC), Combined Annual Training Camps (CATC), and national-level competitions at Thal Sainik Camp.
